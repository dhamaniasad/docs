# TypingMind Plugin

MemoryPlugin offers native integration with TypingMind through our dedicated plugin. TypingMind is a popular alternative interface for various AI models that provides enhanced features and customization options.

<Info>
The TypingMind Plugin content was limited in our source material. This integration is available and supported - check the MemoryPlugin dashboard for specific setup instructions.
</Info>

## What is TypingMind?

TypingMind is a powerful alternative interface for AI models that offers:

- Support for multiple AI providers (OpenAI, Claude, Gemini, etc.)
- Enhanced conversation management
- Custom prompts and templates
- Plugin ecosystem
- Team collaboration features

## Installation

1. Access your MemoryPlugin [Dashboard](https://memoryplugin.com/dashboard)
2. Look for TypingMind integration options
3. Follow the provided setup instructions for connecting MemoryPlugin to your TypingMind instance

## Features

When integrated with TypingMind, MemoryPlugin provides:

- **Persistent Memory**: Remember information across all TypingMind conversations
- **Cross-Model Memory**: Share memories between different AI models in TypingMind
- **Memory Buckets**: Organize memories for different projects or contexts
- **Team Memory**: Share memories across team members (with TypingMind Custom)

## TypingMind Custom for Teams

MemoryPlugin offers special integration with TypingMind Custom, which is often used by teams or businesses. This integration is currently under development.

### For Internal Teams

MemoryPlugin can save time and ensure consistency by providing the ability to:
- Learn and recall important information about your business
- Remember product details and specifications  
- Maintain your preferred writing style and tone
- Build an internal knowledge base that grows automatically over time

### For End-User Applications

For TypingMind Custom instances serving end-users, MemoryPlugin enables:
- Better customer relationships through remembered preferences
- Greater personalization as the AI learns about each user
- Higher engagement since conversations don't start from scratch
- Improved user experience with contextual conversations
